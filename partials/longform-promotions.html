Free spins & game restrictions

Spins may be locked to specific titles at a fixed line bet. Winnings might credit as cash or as bonus funds—each path has different wagering. Check spin expiry; unused spins typically vanish when the clock hits zero.

Wagering requirements (rollover)

Wagering multipliers tell you how much you must bet before bonus winnings become withdrawable cash. A 30x requirement on a $100 bonus implies $3,000 in qualifying bets—not deposits—subject to game weighting. Slots often count 100%; live tables may count less or be excluded. Exceeding max bet while a bonus is active can void the promo—another line to read slowly.

Reload & weekend offers

Reloads reward subsequent deposits. Weekend promos may align with sports calendars or slot tournaments. Stack rules carefully: accepting one deal might block another until completion.

Cashback & risk-sharing promos

Cashback returns a slice of net losses over a period. Caps, game eligibility, and payment as cash vs bonus vary. Treat cashback as a partial rebate, not insurance against poor discipline.

VIP & loyalty

Loyalty points can unlock tiers with faster withdrawals or hosts. Points often accrue from real-money play; bonus play may accrue slower or not at all. Decide whether the perks justify the volume required.
