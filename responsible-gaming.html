Responsible gaming

Gambling should be entertainment, not a way to solve money problems. If it stops being fun, pause and use the tools below.

On-platform tools

Use deposit caps, loss limits, timers, cool-off, and self-exclusion from your account—before you chase losses.

Warning signs

Borrowing to play, hiding bets, or chasing losses? Talk to someone you trust and get help.

4-step self-check

  1. 1Set a strict entertainment budget in cash you can lose.
  2. 2Enable deposit limits day one—even if you “don’t need them.”
  3. 3Track time; alarms beat autoplay loops.
  4. 4If you break rules you set for yourself, take a 7-day break.
