FAQs

How do I verify?

Upload clear ID and a recent bill or bank statement. Finish any selfie step if asked—then withdrawals are smoother.

Why is my cash-out waiting?

Could be bonus playthrough, document review, or the method not matching. The cashier usually spells it out.

Same wallet for jackpots and Megaways?

Usually yes for real money. Bonus funds might be limited to certain games—check the promo.

Can I see RTP?

Open the game info panel—RTP is per title. It’s theoretical, not a promise for your session.
