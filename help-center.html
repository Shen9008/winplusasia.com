How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Open Account → Security (wording varies). Choose an authenticator app or SMS, scan the QR code or enter the one-time code, and store backup codes offline. 2FA reduces takeover risk if your password leaks.

What payment methods work and is there a minimum deposit?

Debit/credit cards, e-wallets, bank transfer, and sometimes crypto depend on your region—check the Cashier. Minimum deposits are shown before you confirm; some welcome offers need a minimum deposit to qualify.

Are there withdrawal fees or limits?

Operators may charge per method, apply daily/monthly caps, or tier limits by verification level. Read their payments FAQ and your cashier—crypto and bank rails often have different speeds and fees.

What is a wagering requirement and how do I track it?

Wagering is how much you need to bet before bonus winnings become withdrawable. Your Bonuses or Active promotions area usually shows progress; only bets on eligible games and stakes within max bet rules count.

I claimed a bonus but it didn’t show up—what should I check?

Confirm you opted in, met the minimum deposit, used an eligible payment method, and are inside the promo window. Wait a few minutes for systems to sync; if it’s still missing, contact support with a screenshot of the offer.

Can I use a VPN or play while travelling?

Many brands prohibit VPNs and block restricted territories. Connecting from a blocked region can void bonuses or lock accounts. Always follow the operator’s terms and the laws where you are physically located.

Do I need an app or can I play in the browser?

Most sites run well in a mobile browser; some offer iOS/Android apps for a more app-like experience. Only download from the operator’s official link or your device’s real app store to avoid fake apps.

What if a game freezes or disconnects mid-round?

Slots and live games usually finish on the server—reopen the title or check bet history. If your balance looks wrong, note the time, game name, and stake, then contact live chat with screenshots if possible.
