FAQs

How do I verify my account?

Complete profile fields, upload government ID and a recent utility bill or bank statement, and follow any selfie/liveness step. Verification reduces withdrawal friction.

Why is my withdrawal pending?

Common causes: active bonus wagering, document review, method mismatch, or fraud checks. Open the cashier note— it usually names the blocker.

Do jackpots and Megaways share wallet balance?

Typically yes for real-money play, but bonus wallets may be restricted. Read the promo card for eligible game IDs.

Can I sort by RTP?

Many lobbies expose RTP in the info panel; some hubs publish curated high-RTP lists. RTP is theoretical and not a guarantee per session.
